Who we are

We help students catch flights they didn't know were boarding.

MD Scholars started as a shared spreadsheet of scholarship deadlines passed between three friends. It's since grown into a full desk — but the job hasn't changed: find the opportunity, then help you actually reach it.

Our journey so far

How the desk grew

Year one

A spreadsheet, shared

Three friends kept passing scholarship deadlines to each other in a group chat. Eventually the group chat became a spreadsheet, and the spreadsheet became the first version of the site.

Year two

The writing desk opens

Students kept asking the same question after finding a listing: "okay, but how do I actually write this essay?" We hired our first two editors to answer it properly.

Year three

Beyond scholarships

Graduates started asking for the same kind of help with job applications and fellowships, so the board expanded beyond pure scholarships to careers and fellowships too.

Today

A full departures board

Hundreds of open opportunities, six writing-desk services, and a growing list of students who've already boarded their flight.

What we believe

Three things we won't compromise on

Every listing is checked

We verify each opportunity against its original source before it's published, and we keep the deadline current — not copied from an old post.

We edit with you, not for you

Our editors sharpen your own story. We won't write an application that doesn't sound like you, because that's the version that actually gets accepted.

No guarantees we can't keep

We won't promise you an admission or a job — nobody honestly can. We will promise your paperwork is ready, complete, and on time.
